### Smart Pay Dashboard

To use the Smart Pay Dashboard, you’ll need the following hardware and software setup: 

- **Internet-enabled device** - desktop, laptop, tablet, or smartphone. 
- **Supported browsers** - latest version of all major browsers. For the best experience, we recommend using  Chrome, Edge, or Safari. 

### In-person payments 

Smart Pay supports multiple types of payment terminals to suit different business needs. The table below outlines the available options and their equipment requirements: 

| **Product**                        | **Requirements**                                                           |
| :--------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Rabo SmartPin with card reader** | Android or iOS device plus a Bluetooth-enabled payment terminal.           |
| **Rabo SmartPin Tap to Pay**       | NFC-enabled Android device or iPhone enrolled via the Smart Pay dashboard. |
| **Fixed or mobile terminal**       | Power supply and stable internet connection (Wi-Fi or 4G).                 |
